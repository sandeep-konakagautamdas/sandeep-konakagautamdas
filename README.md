## 🧬 What I work on

I'm interested in **model-informed drug development (MIDD)** — using mathematical and statistical models to understand how drugs behave in the body, who responds and who doesn't, and how to design smarter trials.

Current focus areas:
- **Population PK (PopPK)** — nonlinear mixed-effects modelling to characterise variability in drug exposure
- **PK/PD modelling** — linking drug concentrations to clinical outcomes
- **PBPK modelling** — mechanistic simulation of drug disposition across tissues and populations
- **Target-mediated drug disposition (TMDD)** — modelling drugs that bind to pharmacological targets
- **Disease areas** - Infections, Oncology, Diabetes mellitus, Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease

### 🔬 [Population PK Modelling of an Oral Analgesic](https://github.com/sandeepkonaka1400/Population-Pharmacokinetic-Modelling)
**Tools:** Pumas (Julia) · Quarto  
Built a full PopPK analysis pipeline for an oral analgesic using nonlinear mixed-effects modelling in Pumas. Includes data assembly, base model development, covariate exploration, and model diagnostics (goodness-of-fit plots, VPC, bootstrap).
